# Conway Electric Branding Notes

## Logo
- Black square icon with a stylized "A" or lightning bolt shape (white on black)
- Bold all-caps wordmark: "CONWAY ELECTRIC" in black, heavy sans-serif (likely Futura or similar geometric bold)
- Two-line stacked layout: "CONWAY" on top, "ELECTRIC" below

## Color Palette
- Primary: Black (#000000) — dominant background and text color
- White (#FFFFFF) — clean backgrounds, text on dark
- Orange/Red (#E8450A or similar) — product accent color (seen on Venice Orange extension cord, product boxes)
- Warm Tan/Cream (#F5EFE0 or similar) — braided cord product color
- Martha Blue (#5B8DB8 or similar) — secondary product accent
- Minimal use of color; mostly black-and-white with bold orange/red accents

## Typography
- All-caps, bold, condensed sans-serif for headings (Futura Bold / similar)
- Clean, modern serif or sans-serif for body text
- Headlines are large, confident, and direct (e.g., "CHARGE UP TO 7X FASTER")
- No decorative fonts — purely utilitarian and industrial

## Aesthetic
- Industrial / utilitarian / American-made
- Bold, confident, no-nonsense
- High contrast (black on white, white on black)
- Product photography: clean white/neutral backgrounds
- Tone: direct, technical, proud of craftsmanship

## App Branding Translation
- App name: "Conway Electric Power Monitor"
- Background: Off-white / cream (#F5F0E8) for light mode feel, or deep black (#0D0D0D) for dark
- Primary accent: Conway Orange (#E8450A) for active/warning states
- Secondary accent: Martha Blue (#5B8DB8) for charging state
- Typography: Bold all-caps condensed headers, clean body text
- Logo: Use Conway Electric logo mark (black square with stylized icon) + wordmark
